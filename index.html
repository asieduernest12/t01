Ride Recording

The simplest ride recoding feature allows you to record your rides, keep track of your stats and allows you to transfer all your data to Strava once complete. Corrdinate or locate near by elliptigo bikers with the map trackride

Find GOGO Points earnings of your interest and see your progress through every completed requirement. Each task has specific details on how it can be accomplished if you wish to work on it.

About Us

Core is a platform that focuses on two main things, ride recording and GOGO Points earning. It is intended to be simple and easy to use for all elliptigo riders without much struggle or issues. This platfrom was created by a group of students from he university of Iowa.
